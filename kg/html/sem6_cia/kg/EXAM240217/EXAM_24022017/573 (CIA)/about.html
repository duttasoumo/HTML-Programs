About "Get Caught Reading" !!

Get Caught reading is a cafe bookhouse located in a small town in the foothills of the Himalayas. We primarily aim at promoting the habit of reading as it is being taken over by television and video games. It is becoming a dying habit which needs a breath of fresh air. We also organize book launches and workshops for children.

This is a small endeavour from my side, as a book enthusiast and a reader, to make this world a better place to live in. Let's connect & spread good thoughts.
